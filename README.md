📚 Online Bookstore Sales Analysis | SQL

📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze sales data from an online bookstore and generate actionable business insights. By querying customer, order, and product data, the project uncovers sales trends, purchasing behavior, inventory performance, and revenue patterns that support informed business decisions.
The project highlights how SQL can transform raw transactional data into meaningful reports for business analysis.

🎯 Business Problem
Online bookstores generate large volumes of transactional data, making it challenging to identify revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and inventory performance through raw tables alone.
This project addresses these challenges by analyzing sales and customer data to answer important business questions related to revenue, customer behavior, and product performance.

🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to use SQL to extract, analyze, and interpret business data that helps stakeholders:
Monitor sales performance
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Identify best-selling books
Track revenue trends
Support inventory and sales decisions through data-driven insights

📊 Key Analysis Performed
Customer purchase analysis
Revenue analysis
Best-selling book identification
Genre-wise sales analysis
Inventory performance evaluation
Customer order history analysis
Order and sales trend reporting

❓Business Questions Answered

This project helps answer questions such as:
Which books generate the highest revenue?
Who are the most valuable customers?
Which genres perform best?
What are the monthly sales trends?
Which books require inventory attention?
What purchasing patterns can be observed among customers?
Which orders contribute the most to overall revenue?

💡 Key Business Insights
Identified top-selling books contributing the highest revenue.
Analyzed customer purchasing behavior to recognize high-value customers.
Compared sales performance across different book categories.
Evaluated order trends to understand business growth.
Generated reports that support inventory planning and sales optimization.
Simplified business reporting through efficient SQL queries.
